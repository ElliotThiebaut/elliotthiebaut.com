    Star Wars - The Game

    Mini-game developed by myself in CSS/JS (for the frontend) and PHP (for the backend).

    The main features are: menu, the character that moves (with a physics system for gravity) up, down and sideways, the character is able to shoot lasers to destroy enemies (with a scoring system), the speed that evolves as the time goes by and the recording of the best score of all players with their nickname.

    The game is playable online: game.elliotthiebaut.com

    Cercle Cyclope

    As part of my work as Webmaster at ClairmabStudio, I had the opportunity to create a WordPress website from scratch for the company Cercle Cyclope.

    I made mock-ups and designed the UX and UI of the site according to the client's needs and requests. I also proceeded to the integration of the latter, using WordPress as a CMS so that the site administrators could regularly publish content in the form of articles. The E-commerce part of the site was done with Woocomerce, while taking into account the difficulties of migrating the active client base from the old site.

    Finally, I took care of the online publishing, hosting and domain names for the client.

    EcoTrip

    Design of a website for a travel agency. The concept behind the site is to offer the user a service that sets up original eco-responsible holidays for users.

    Thus, through the site, UX has been thought to make the navigation and booking of the trip fluid, natural, simple, intuitive and fast. The user enter some information, and learn about the proposed activities and chooses the one that interests him.

    The illustrations were made by the classmates present on the project with me, while I took care of the web design.

    See more

    Mobile game

    As part of my studies, I was challenged to make a mobile game in the Star Wars theme in 5 hours maximum. So I made this "beat them all" type game.

    The player chooses his side of the force when he connects for the first time, then, accompanied by a guide in the form of Yoda, will carry out single-player missions where he has to cross levels and beat bosses. He can advance from level to level by discovering the story through cinematic movies. When he feels ready, he can play multiplayer where he can fight for the conquest of several galaxies against players who have chosen the opposite side of the force.
    There is also a shop where you can unlock new characters and buy helpful items for real money.
